Poetry

A collection of poems inspired by the cosmos, astronomy, and the wonders of the universe.

Cosmic Wanderer

Through endless night, the stars align Whispers of light from distant time Galaxies spin in cosmic dance While we observe, entranced The universe speaks in silent tongue Of ancient songs forever sung We seek the answers, search the sky And wonder why, and wonder why

Observatory Nights

Beneath the dome, I stand alone With telescope trained on the unknown The darkness brings a different sight Where photons travel through the night Each point of light, a story told Of stellar births and deaths of old The cosmos vast, the mysteries deep While mortals dream, the heavens keep

Stardust

We are but dust from distant stars Forged in cores and scattered far Carbon, oxygen, iron too Elements that make me and you The universe looks back through our eyes At galaxies and endless skies Connected through this cosmic thread From the living to the long since dead
